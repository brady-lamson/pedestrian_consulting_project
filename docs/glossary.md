| Term / Initialism | Full Term | Definition |
|---|---|---|
| CDOT | Colorado Department of Transportation | State agency responsible for Colorado’s highway system. |
| SGM | SGM Engineering, Inc. | Colorado-based engineering and surveying consulting firm. |
| ADT | Average Daily Traffic | Average number of vehicles passing a point on a roadway per day. |
| AADT | Annual Average Daily Traffic | Average daily traffic volume calculated over an entire year. |
| PEDs | Pedestrians | People traveling on foot. |
| PPH | Pedestrians Per Hour | Number of pedestrians crossing or passing a location per hour. |
| VPD | Vehicles Per Day | Total number of vehicles passing a point on a roadway per day. |
| RRFB | Rectangular Rapid Flashing Beacon | High-intensity flashing lights used to alert drivers to pedestrian crossings. |
| MOE | Measure(s) of Effectiveness | Metrics used to evaluate transportation system performance. |
| LOS | Level of Service | Grading system (A–F) describing traffic flow quality and delay at an intersection or roadway. |
| TWSC | Two-Way Stop Control | Intersection control where the minor road has stop signs but the major road does not. |
| NBL | Northbound Left | Left-turn movement for northbound traffic. |
| NBR | Northbound Right | Right-turn movement for northbound traffic. |
| NBT | Northbound Through | Through movement for northbound traffic. |
| Int Delay, s/veh | Intersection Delay (seconds per vehicle) | Average delay experienced by each vehicle passing through an intersection. |
| HCM | Highway Capacity Manual | Standard reference published by the Transportation Research Board for analyzing roadway capacity and operations. |
| CMF | Crash Modification Factor | Multiplicative factor estimating the change in crash frequency after implementing a safety treatment. |
| DHV | Design Hour Volume | The 30th highest annual hourly traffic volume reported as % of AADT. |
| DVMT| Daily Vehicle Miles Travelled | The average daily vehicle miles traveled for the segment. (Annual Average Daily Traffic (AADT) multiplied by the length of the segment).|
